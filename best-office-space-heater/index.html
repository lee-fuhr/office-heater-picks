Quick answer

The Dyson Hot + Cool Jet Focus is the best office heater for most people thanks to its powerful yet quiet performance and bladeless safety design.

The Lasko 754200 Ceramic Heater is a strong runner-up for those wanting a more traditional ceramic heater, while the Honeywell HF-910 offers exceptional value for budget shoppers.

Cold offices can sap your focus and productivity, but the right space heater makes all the difference. The best models warm up fast, run safely for hours, and won’t drown out your Zoom calls with fan noise. With dozens of options on the market, we’ve dug through expert reviews and owner feedback to find the heaters that deliver consistent comfort without the safety compromises. When choosing an office heater, key factors are size of the space, noise level, safety features like tip-over shutoff and cool-touch exteriors, and how much control you want over temperature and timer settings. Ceramic heaters are the most common and affordable; infrared and oil-filled models offer quieter, more even heat but tend to be pricier. We considered all types to cover different desk setups and budgets.

Best for specific needs

Best for open-plan offices

In a shared workspace, noise and safety are top concerns. The Dyson Hot + Cool Jet Focus runs so quietly it won’t bother neighbors, and its bladeless design eliminates burn risks. Its wide airflow keeps a personal zone comfortable without blowing papers off your desk. Our pick: Dyson Hot + Cool Jet Focus.

Best for small cubicles

When desk space is at a premium, the Lasko MyHeat plugs directly into the wall and stays out of the way. It uses infrared heat that feels warm instantly and makes no fan noise. It’s perfect for warming your hands and feet in a compact cubicle without upsetting nearby coworkers. Our pick: Lasko MyHeat.

Best for private offices

If you want to heat an entire private room without temperature swings, the Vornado AVH10 uses its signature air circulation to maintain an even warmth. Its metal construction and robust thermostat make it a long-lasting choice for daily use. It’s especially good for larger rooms where basic ceramic heaters struggle. Our pick: Vornado AVH10.

Our verdict

Which heater you choose ultimately depends on your office size, noise tolerance, and budget. For most people, the Dyson Hot + Cool Jet Focus delivers the best combination of silence, safety, and performance, but its high cost isn’t for everyone. The Lasko 754200 is a wallet-friendly alternative that still offers great control, while the Honeywell HF-910 is the top pick if you’re on a shoestring. If you need something ultra-compact, grab the Lasko MyHeat. And for whole-room warmth in a private space, the Vornado AVH10 is the smart upgrade. Whichever you pick, make sure your heater has an auto shutoff and tip-over protection – those features are non-negotiable in an office setting.
